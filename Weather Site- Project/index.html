Enjojing the Nature in Otavalo

by Someone
November 2020
One of the best Quito day trips on offer, Otavalo is an indigenous stronghold, famous for the Otavalo market, an everyday occurrence that’s said to be the largest in South America. Tourists flock in just to see this market in action. The city is just a couple of hours in a bus North of the capital, which is what makes Otavalo market such a popular Quito day trip (book here!), but there’s a lot more to see around the city than inside it, so at the very least a 2-night stay in Otavalo would be recommended if you have the time. The area is also home to one of the best waterfalls in Northern Ecuador – Peguche waterfall.
